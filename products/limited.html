Heparan sulfate polymers in nature extend to hundreds of sugar residues. The complex sulfation pattern and the chain length is believed to contribute to the functional diversity of heparan sulfate. We present to you the 18-mer heparan sulfate library, reflecting the length and complexity of natural heparan sulfate, a cost-effective resource for application of heparan sulfate in research.
